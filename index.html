How I work

Flexible end-to-end process

My design approach is built on direct communication with the team and understanding the task context. I enjoy structuring data, building systems, and writing documentation, while avoiding processes that don't bring value. If quality is the priority — conduct research and run workshops. If the deadline is yesterday, prioritize and cut the scope — not the corners.
