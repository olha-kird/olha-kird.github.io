How I work

Flexible end-to-end process

My design approach is built on direct communication with the team and understanding the task context. I enjoy structuring data, building systems, and writing documentation, while avoiding processes that don't bring value. If quality is the priority — conduct research and run workshops. If the deadline is yesterday — prioritize and cut the scope, not the corners.

About me

Designer with engineering thinking

My design journey started in 2020, co-founding a startup with university friends. Despite launching during the pandemic, our restaurant management product attracted B2B customers and was later acquired by one of the most prominent tech companies in Ukraine.

My professional experience is focused primarily on complex B2B systems. Agency work taught me to quickly grasp product context and find an individual approach to each problem, while in-house product experience added continuous discovery and cross-functional collaboration. My software engineering degree helps me navigate technical constraints and design in code with AI.

My hobbies include video games, music, mobile photography, and sometimes hiking. I am also an active member of several design communities in Berlin and use every opportunity to exchange knowledge and make new connections.
