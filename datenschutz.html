There is no cross-site tracking or advertising, and anonymous visitors receive no persistent profile. The legal basis is our legitimate interest in improving the site (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR). You can opt out at any time by enabling “Do Not Track” in your browser, which this site respects. See PostHog's privacy policy.
